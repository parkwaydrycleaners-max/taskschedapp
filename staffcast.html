→ Rain Demand Redistribution
When ON: ~78% of demand lost to moderate/heavy rain is recovered — 52% shifts to the next open day, 26% to Saturday. The remaining ~22% is permanently lost. When OFF: all rain-lost demand is permanently lost (the rainy day still shows reduced pieces either way).
→ Heat Demand Redistribution
When ON: ~68% of demand lost to extreme heat is recovered — 48% shifts to the next cooler open day, 20% to Saturday. The remaining ~32% is permanently lost. When OFF: all heat-lost demand is permanently lost (the hot day still shows reduced pieces either way). Rain takes priority if a day has both.
