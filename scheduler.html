Accepted formats:

1. POS Sales Report (auto-detected):
 Sales Report By Category
 Drop   Dry Pcs   Dry Amt   Laund Pcs ...

Paste the full report including both left-side (Drop through Press Pcs) and right-side (Press Amt through Total Amt) pages. The app will automatically merge them.

2. Wide CSV (categories as columns):
Date, Food, Beverage, Retail
2025-04-01, 5200, 1800, 900

3. Long CSV (one row per category):
Date, Category, Amount
2025-04-01, Food, 5200

Dates can be YYYY-MM-DD, MM/DD/YYYY, YYYY/MM/DD, or similar.

📉 Minimum Sales Threshold

Days with total sales below this amount are treated as anomalies (e.g. one-off transaction on a mostly-closed day) and excluded from forecast calculations.

🛑 Closed Days

Select days of the week when you are closed. Forecast will show $0 and 0 hours for these days.

🎉 Holidays (Closed)

Add dates when you are closed (MM-DD format). These dates will show $0 / 0 hours in forecasts every year.

📅 Seasonal Events

Add recurring events that affect sales (e.g. prom season, back-to-school). The app learns actual impact from your historical data.

📂 Sales Categories

Categories are auto-detected from imported data. You can also add them manually.
